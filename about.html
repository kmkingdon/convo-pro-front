What does Convo-pro do?

Do you struggle thinking of interesting and thought provoking questions? Do you ever blank on what to ask next on your first date? Convo-pro quickly generates questions to spice up any conversation. In quick conversating mode, Convo-pro will supply engaging questions seemlessly. If you want more control, use advanced conversating to set intimacy level or toggle on naughty questions. You can even save your favorite questions for later! Whatever your conversation needs, Convo-pro has you covered.
